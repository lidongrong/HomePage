Dongrong Li

 

Hello and Welcome! I am Dongrong Li, currently a Ph.D. student at Department of Statistics, Chinese University of Hong Kong

My research focuses on developing methodologies and scalable algorithms that are applicable to statistical machine learning and inference problems with complicated structure.

Currently, I am interested in electronic health record data (EMR) analysis and scalable MCMC algorithm for large-scale Bayesian computation.

Before joining CUHK, I received a B.S. from Department of Mathematics, Sun Yat-sen University.

Research Interests

  • Statistical Machine Learning

  • Missing Data Problem

  • Parallel Computing and Scalable Bayesian Inference

  • Medical Data Analysis

  • Markov Chain Monte Carlo
